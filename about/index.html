The Upsdell Trivia

Upsdell was called Lugard and St.Dunstan's before 1946!

Upsdell is one of the four houses in King George V School which was named after G. Upsdell, who came to KGV in 1925 from Queen's College. He was appointed Principal in 1935 overseeing the move to our present site in Homantin. He remained as principal until the school closed in 1940. He and his wife were imprisoned in Stanley during the occupation. They both survived but he died in 1946 as a result of the effects of these five years.

What is this site?

This site is the website for Upsdell. This domain has been up for a long time, but this particular design was made in 2008.

The background of the website is the bubble chamber, symbolizing the triumph of physics.
